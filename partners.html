“Stronger together: Building circular fashion ecosystems.”

At Rewear Closet, we believe in the power of partnership — from local thrift shops and global fashion brands to design schools and sustainability-driven orgs.

We’re collaborating with institutions that share our vision: to embed circularity into education, commerce, and everyday style.

Whether you're a retail giant looking to get real about your green goals, or a fashion school ready to prep students for a post-fast fashion world — we’re ready to build with you.

“Giving tomorrow’s designers the tools to design for impact.”

FOR DESIGN SCHOOLS / STUDENTS

We’re partnering with fashion schools to bring real-world circular economy tools into the classroom.

Through Rewear Closet, students can:

  • ✔ Upload and digitally style their collections
  • ✔ Resell past garments safely and locally
  • ✔ Promote their brand with in-app stores
  • ✔ Learn the value of sustainable design through tech

Bonus: We provide schools with analytics, insights, and opportunities to integrate Rewear Closet into coursework and capstone projects.
